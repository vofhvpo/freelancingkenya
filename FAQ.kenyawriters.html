Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What services do you offer?

We provide a range of high-quality freelance writing, editing, and consultation services across various fields, including academic research, professional business writing, creative projects, and resume optimization.

2. Where are you located?

Our headquarters is located in Nairobi, Kenya, at Westlands Amenza Plaza, Third Floor, Room 19A. However, we offer online services globally and have a dedicated team that operates 24/7 to accommodate clients from all time zones.

3. What makes you different?

We pride ourselves on a transparent hiring and vetting process, experienced and certified professionals, competitive and flexible pricing, as well as our commitment to continuous support. We’re available 24/7 and prioritize quality and accuracy in every project.

4. How do I place an order?

You can place an order through our website by navigating to the order form or contacting our support team directly. Provide details such as the type of service, deadline, and any specific instructions or resources you want our team to follow.

5. How do you ensure quality?

Quality is our top priority. Our team comprises certified professionals with specialized expertise in various fields. All work undergoes a thorough quality check by experienced editors before delivery to ensure it meets your expectations and our high standards.

6. What are the payment methods?

We accept various payment options, including PayPal, Skrill, Payoneer, and bank transfers. For larger orders, we offer flexible payment plans to make the process as convenient as possible for our clients.

7. What is your refund policy?

Client satisfaction is important to us. If for any reason you’re unsatisfied with the work, we offer revisions to address your concerns. In cases where revisions don’t meet your standards, we provide a partial or full refund, depending on the specifics of the project.

8. How can I join your team?

We welcome skilled freelancers to join our team! To apply, visit our Careers page and submit your resume, portfolio, and a brief cover letter explaining your expertise. Our hiring team will review your application and reach out if there’s a match.

9. How is freelancer payment structured?

Freelancers are paid per project or page, depending on the type and complexity of work. Beginner rates start at $4 per page, while experienced writers can earn up to $12 per page. We issue payments bi-monthly via your preferred payment method.

10. Do you offer training?

Yes, we offer onboarding sessions for new freelancers and provide ongoing support through our dedicated freelancer support team. We also host regular training workshops to help freelancers enhance their skills and grow professionally.

11. What certifications do I need?

Freelancers don’t require specific licenses to join us. However, possessing relevant certifications, like a CPRW (Certified Professional Resume Writer) or equivalent, can improve your eligibility and rate. All freelance work adheres to Kenya’s employment and taxation guidelines, and freelancers should ensure they comply with local regulations.

12. How do you handle confidentiality?

We prioritize the privacy and security of our client’s information. All freelancers and staff members sign confidentiality agreements to ensure that any sensitive information shared is handled with utmost discretion.

13. Do you have a quality policy?

Yes, we have a strict quality policy that each team member follows. Our editorial team reviews each project to ensure it meets all required standards before submission.

14. What about technical support issues?

If you experience any technical difficulties on our platform or have issues with our service, our support team is available 24/7 to assist. Contact us through live chat, email, or phone, and we will resolve the issue promptly.

15. How can I reach customer support?

You can reach our customer support team through email, live chat on our website, or phone. We’re available 24/7 to address any questions or concerns.

16. Do you offer a mobile app?

We are in the process of developing a mobile app to provide better accessibility for our clients and freelancers. For now, our website is mobile-friendly and accessible on any device.

17. How can I give feedback?

Your feedback is valuable to us. You can provide feedback directly through our website’s feedback form or by contacting our support team. We use feedback to continuously improve our services.

18. Can I request a specific freelancer?

Yes, if you have previously worked with a freelancer and would like to request them again, you can indicate this preference when placing an order. We’ll do our best to accommodate your request based on availability.
